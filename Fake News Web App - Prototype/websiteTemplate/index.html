Fake News Detection

Get started below to check authenticity of news articles

Why Fake News Detection

Fake news detection is vital to prevent the spread of false information that can mislead the public, disrupt democracy, and create panic. It safeguards accurate information, protects reputations, and helps maintain social order in an increasingly digital and interconnected world. Detecting and countering fake news also foster critical thinking skills and promote responsible online behavior.

Implementation Insights

Through sophisticated algorithms, the app detects linguistic patterns consistent with fake news. This automated analysis offers an additional layer of scrutiny, flagging potential misinformation.

Project Details

In this project, we integrated a machine learning model designed to detect fake news into a web application using Django/Flask. By leveraging the power of Python's machine learning libraries and frameworks, we created an intelligent system capable of identifying and flagging potentially deceptive news articles. By providing a user-friendly interface through the web application, users can input news articles for analysis, and the model's results are presented in a clear and accessible format. This implementation effectively combines cutting-edge technology with web development to address the critical issue of fake news propagation in the digital age.
